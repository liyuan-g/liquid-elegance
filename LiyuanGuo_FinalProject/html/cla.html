Classic Cocktails

Classic cocktails are the cornerstone of mixology, celebrated for their simplicity, balance, and enduring appeal. These iconic drinks often feature a harmonious blend of base spirits, mixers, and garnishes, showcasing the artistry of cocktail craftsmanship.

Old Fashioned

A timeless classic, the Old Fashioned combines whiskey, sugar, bitters, and a touch of orange zest.

Negroni

The Negroni is a sophisticated blend of gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth, garnished with orange peel.

Dry Martini

A symbol of elegance, the Dry Martini features gin, dry vermouth, and a classic olive garnish.
